a Black neighborhood in several forms

By Quana



quiet when the sun rises

size 3, child’s feet

school bus screeches

barrettes clack together

they match the shoes

a tended garden

tender-headed grandchild

A/C will not be let out

down the street

2 grocery stores closed

40 minutes feat just so the deli man can be rude

chess on the sidewalk

“your play uncle winning”

he always cheat though

an English teacher

uses Ebonics brazenly

while selling cold cups

a white boy tries to trap your older sister

just like a blonde girl and a D1 athlete

he doesn’t know she’s been kicking with Darius down the street since 9th grade

his family just moved in

he smokes weed while his daddy’s a cop

“I think I belong here”

his momma teaches yoga and loves the whole foods 40 minutes away

Quana is a non-binary lesbian from the 504 & writes about the revolution, lesbianism, and being a Black Native. They do a lot of harm reduction work & they are a birth-worker.
