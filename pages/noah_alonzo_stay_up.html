Stay Up

By Noah Alonzo


    There’s something about seeing the sunrise

    after the constant wandering of my mind

    Hear the morning birds

    Sing while looking for worms

    In which I now wonder if they have the same

    for desert

    from the night I wondered if I really deserve

    Such fairness of what life offers

    As it contradicts itself in its own manor

    There are no standards or conducts on how to

    live with such a cancer

    Move as you please or stand absolutely still

    As you can see it’s all the same thrill

    A nightmare like this feels far too real

    So I ask, why would I stay up any longer?

Noah Alonzo is a writer, actor, and musician from Chicago, IL. He found his creative side through journaling lyrics and poetry into now eight notebooks. Noah starred as Oz in the award winning film Da Big Zip (2020), and releases music under the rap moniker Slurname.
